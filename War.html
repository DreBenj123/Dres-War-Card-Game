WAR!

This Is A Simple Repetitive Game Based On Luck!

Rules:

  1. 2 player game.
  2. Each Player is dealt a 26 cards from the 52 set deck.
  3. Players will flip the top card of their deck face up and whichever Player has the higher value card wins and both cards are taken by the winner.
  4. If the cards in play are of equal value then its War! Each Player will take a card off the deck face down, then another face up and whoever has the higher value will win all cards. (6 cards)
  5. This will continue until all cards are owned by one Player!
